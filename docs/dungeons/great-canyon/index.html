Another dungeon without a boss fight - The fugitive arc of the story begins after clearing this dungeon.
Once you enter Pokemon Square, you won't be able to visit camps, go to other dungeons, or take on bulletin board jobs.
If your stock of wands, seeds, orbs, and so on are low, consider collecting some items before clearing this dungeon.
Don't forget to use Dojo Tickets if you want to learn a specific level up move before the fugitive arc.
