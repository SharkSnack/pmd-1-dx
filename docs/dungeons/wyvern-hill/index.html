Optional post-game dungeon featuring lots of Dragon types, and a mediocre reward.
Enemies besides Totodile and Ludicolo are weak to Ice type attacks, so Blizzard is very effective here.
Exeggcute (Strong Foe) can know extremely dangerous moves like Barrage, Bullet Seed, and Uproar,
so bring lots of Tiny Reviver Seeds if your party's stats are still on the low side.
