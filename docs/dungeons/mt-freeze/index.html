Unlock Uproar Forest

Final dungeon of the fugitive arc, and this time there isn't a boss fight at the end.
Once you clear this dungeon, you'll return to your Rescue Team base and can access Pokemon Square again.
Hail and Sandstorm (via Pupitar) weather both appear, so consider bringing orbs or healing items as a counter.
Pineco can know Bug Bite, so use ranged attacks to keep your Oran Berries and Tiny Reviver Seeds safe.
Glalie can know Sheer Cold which instantly KOs a target, so don't let it act when adjacent.

  • Cannot recruit during the first clear.
